Controls Movement: WASD or IJKL (Up, Down, Left, Right)
Grab the Sacred Pumpkin: Spacebar
Aim and Shoot Fire Blasts:
Mouse Pointer and Left-Click
Recharge Fire Blasts: Automatic after 3.5 seconds
Respawn:
Automatic after 5 seconds
Objective You and your friends are having a spooky party...but noone brought pumpkins! Rival Phantoms have had the same idea, but pumpkins are limited! Be the first team to bring 10 pumpkins back to your graveyard, but be wary. All phantoms can shoot deadly fireballs, and lugging pumpkins around will slow you down and keep your fire ball hands full!
